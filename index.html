How It Works

Forming the perfect set of teams is easy with MatchKit. Using our cloud-based tool, follow these three simple steps.

Tell us About Your Team Members

Use our survey tool to collect information from your participants. We only ask the questions you need. If you already have data, you can upload your file and start from there.

Define your Perfect Team

Tell us what a perfect team looks like to you. We also provide suggestions from leading team-performance research to make sure yours is the best.

See and Share Results

We show you results graphically, allowing you to easily check the results. You can export your results in a variety of formats.
